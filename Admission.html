Registeration

Parents wishing to admit a child to The Zaki's Academy should first register him/her for an admission test by completing the Admission Form available at the school office, after which they will receive an appointment card for the test and interview.

  • Registration fee is charged at the time of registration and is non-refundable.
  • Registration does not in any way confirm or guarantee admission.
  • Information provided on the Application Form should be correct.

The Zaki's Academy has a specific and regular admission policy throughout the country which is communicated to the parents by each region. It is mandatory to adhere to the policy.

Test and Interview

A list displaying the names of the selected candidates will be put up on the noticeboard of the relevant school on the given date and letters will be dispatched to the parents. At the time of admission, a non-refundable admission fee, a refundable security deposit, and two months’ tuition fee has to be paid. Admission remains provisional and is not confirmed unless the following documents are produced:At the Playgroup, Nursery, and Kindergarten levels, an informal assessment is conducted. Generally, the reading ability of the child, vocabulary, recognition of words, and concept of colours and numbers is assessed. The confidence and motor skills and the appearance and conduct of the child are also taken into consideration – how the child sits, walks and talks. At the Class 1 and 2 levels, a written test in English, Urdu and Mathematics is mandatory. In addition to the assessment, a formal interview is conducted with both the parents. Merit is the sole criterion for admission.
